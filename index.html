Web Projects

  • The Inspirada App is a digital space for users to find and share blog posts about women in tech. The objective is to motivate more women and girls to pursue a career in tech by providing role models they can relate to.

  • Technology: JS | React | Ruby | Sinatra | SQL | CSS3 |
  • Yucaju-App

  • The Yucaju-App was created to find a solution to the difficulty to communicate in the island of Puerto Rico during the aftermath of a Hurricane. With this app, users can find and share the location of resources nearest to their specific "barrio" neighborhood. Since the main tool of communication used during hurricane Maria was cellphones connected to wifi hot-spots, the design for this app is meant for mobile devices.

  • Technology: JS | React | Node | Express | Mongo | Mongoose | CSS3 | Socrata Open Data API
